Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Delivery times vary by region: India (5–10 days), USA/Canada (7–14 days), Europe (10–18 days), Australia (12–20 days). See our full Shipping Policy.

Are the crystals genuine?

Yes. Every bracelet uses certified authentic gemstones. Natural colour and texture variations are expected and are signs of authenticity. We do not use dyed glass or synthetic imitations.

Can I return my order?

We offer a 14-day return window on all non-customised items. See our Returns Policy for full details.

What is numerology? Is it scientific?

Numerology is an ancient belief system that assigns meaning to numbers derived from names and dates. It is not a scientifically validated discipline. Our readings are provided for entertainment and self-insight purposes only and do not constitute professional advice.

How is my personal data used?

Your data is collected only as needed to process your order or reading and is never sold to third parties. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details. We comply with GDPR and CCPA.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Razorpay (for UPI, Net Banking, and Indian payment methods). All payments are processed via PCI-DSS compliant gateways.

Do I have to pay customs or import duties?

International orders may be subject to import duties or taxes set by your country's customs authority. These are the buyer's responsibility. We clearly mark all parcels with accurate customs values.
